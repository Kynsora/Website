Top Earning Players for Korea and USA

Korean's Income

Althought Korea has top performing players, their highest earning player only racked in approximately $1.4mil.
This seems like a lot of money but when comparing to the income of the highest earner in the U.S, we see that that the difference is huge with the highest income being $3.4mil.
The difference is by a whole 2 million, the reason behide this is because of popularity and status. The U.S has better market value for the teams and players.
Unfortunately even though Korea has most of the talent, they are being seriously undervalued, most try to come to the U.S to make a better income.

U.S.A's Income

With $3.4 mil as the highest earning player from the U.S, that does not mean that the U.S necessarily wins the most tournaments. Depending on the game the U.S has a lot of titles, but overall Korea has won the most in the history of E-sports.
The U.S has its main income stream coming from Fortnite, while Korea owns the market for League of Legends.
The reason for the big difference is due to prize pools for each game. In fornite the prize pool was $5mil at one point, however you have to divide that by 3 or 1 depending on the amount of players. Unlike League of Legends it is a smaller prize pool with more team players up to 5-6.
that makes the earnings really skewed.
